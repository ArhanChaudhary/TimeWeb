Ideation
My journey creating TimeWeb began on October, 2019. I was an 8th grade student at middle school in Fremont, California. In my science class, my teacher played a motivational video about procrastination. I had seen the video before, in fact. It featured a simple graph of daily work to complete over time for an assignment. Though I found it entertaining, I didn't think much of it. However, after rewatching the video in class, I had an idea: what if you could code a similar graph system for your own work?
